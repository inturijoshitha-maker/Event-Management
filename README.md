Managing campus events and registrations manually often leads to delays, errors, and poor engagement. Students face difficulties in discovering events, registering, and tracking their participation status.
A centralized platform is needed to simplify event registration, provide real-time updates, and enhance student engagement.
The Campus Event Navigator Registration Hub aims to streamline event announcements, enable online registration, and track attendance efficiently.
